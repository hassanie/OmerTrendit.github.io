Token Valuation


Generally for an item to increase in value there has to be a high demand, limited supply, and users willing to use the product. Trendit's core functionality will be built around maximizing Trend Token use as described below:


Creating Demand:


TrenditStudios will increase token demand by offering payment and advertising discounts for payments made using Trend Tokens. For example a clothing company is charged 100,000$ by Trendit to promote their new trend. If the company chooses to pay in Trend Tokens, TrenditStudios will offer them a 20% discount, saving them 20,000$.


Decreasing Supply:


Everytime payments are made using Trend Tokens our payment smart contract, part of the DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization), will automatically burn half of the tokens used in the payment. This process will continue until only 100 Million trend tokens remain in circulation.


Acquiring Users:


Every week the DAO will calculate the total number of new pledges each user accumulates, and pay them a share from the available user acquisition tokens. Once the platform reaches a profitability threshold, Trendit will start paying users 50% of platform profits in Ethereum along with Trend Tokens. Rewarding people has been a proven method to acquire a strong user base.

Trendit Roadmap


  • Company Established SEP 2017
    TrenditStudios goals and the founding team were established!
  • Platform Design OCT 2017
    Trendit's primary functionalities and design goals were established. Also the token model and it's distribution strategy was finalized.
  • Build Community NOV-DEC 2017
    Trendit was announced publically. Bounty programs were organized to start building our user base.
  • Initial Coin Offering FEB 2018
    TrenditStudios will launch a 1 month long Initial Coin Offering through a crowdsale.
  • App Development Q1 2018
    Complete the basic development of a web application with an in-house development team. Start working on mobile versions of the application.
  • Beta-Launch Q2 2018
    Beta launch of the web and mobile platforms. Start letting investors and early adopters test and discover bugs on the developed applications. Pay early adopters with Trend Tokens!
  • Marketing Q3/Q4 2018
    Use marketing portion of crowdsale funds to drive users to our platform through social media interaction and mass advertising.
  • Creator Payments Q1/Q2 2019
    Start paying content creators dividends in both Ethereum and Trend Tokens. Trend Tokens will continue to be paid out until user acquisition tokens are depleted.
