  • Platform

  • What is Trendit?

    A platform for viral content that rewards users and provides Big data Analytics powered by AI services to businesses all on the blockchain.

  • Who are Trend Setters?

    Another name for content creator, any user of the platform that add posts, links, images, contests etc.

  • How is Trendit different from other social media platforms?

    Trendit is different from current social media because it focuses on trends, events and challenges. It’s purpose is less on social communication but rather interactions of individuals or groups on products, news or any other content.

  • When will the alpha platform be made available?

    The alpha platform is targeted for End of Q1 2018. An extensive roadmap will be released upon the completion of the crowdsale.

  • Can anyone participate in the alpha?

    Yes, you will just need to register an account on our site!

  • Business Model

  • What are some use cases for trendit?

    Trend setters will get paid in TND for sharing their content depending on the traction it gathers TND will also be used as means of transaction for users who would like to use the analytics and promotion services provided by Trendit. Any user can earn rewards in TND with their content. Content can be as simple as a picture of your breakfast to a detailed challenge for your friends/followers or everyone to run 10KM every week.

  • How will TND holders (aka. Hodlers) benefit?

    When users/enterprises pay using TND to use the platform services, half of the payment will be burnt until a total of 100 million token remain in circulation. This will positively impact the token value.

  • What happens when TND runs out and users can't pay in TND anymore?

    Using TND over any other currency on the platform allows users to get a discount on the payment. This will encourage them to use TND as a means of payment. Trendit will allow users to buy TND for using as payments.

  • What about competition?

    Currently there are very few if any platforms that are a direct competition to Trendit. We plan to be the first in the market and utilize that to our advantage.

  • Tokensale

  • What are TND tokens??

    Trendit (TND) are ERC20 standard tokens created on the Ethereum blockchain. They are utility tokens for the transaction of trends on the Trendit platform.

  • What is the total and circulating supply?

    There is a total of 200 Million tokens available. 120 Million tokens will be in circulation.

  • What is the distribution of tokens?

    60% of the tokens will be allocated for ICO. Remaining 40% will be reserved by Trendit.

    The reserved are for User Payment and acquisition to start the platform, Bounties, Airdrops, Advisors and as contingency budgets for additional resources as needed

  • When is the ICO?

    The Initial Coin Offering will take place on Feb 8, 2018.

  • Is there a whitelist for the ICO and Pre-sale

    Yes, there will be two seperate whitelist for each sale. An annoucement will be made on all social media about it.

  • Company

  • Why did we start Trendit?

    We started Trendit because current social media sites don’t give power to the users who use the platform. Additionally, the data users generate are kept away and sold to data agencies who then sell to other lucrative parties. This helps social media and data agencies to win while the users of the platform aren’t rewarded in any way. Our vision is to create a platform that is fair to all users in it through the use of blockchain technology.
